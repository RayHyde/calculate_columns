Calculate columns quickly

Just fill in the wrapper width and number of columns, then play with the gutter widths (arrow keys are quickest) and presto, you'll find the non-decimal column widths in pixels.

Just for the heck of it you can choose percentages. Not the greatest thing since sliced bread, but hey, I had fun making it!
